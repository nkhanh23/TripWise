# TripWise Design System

## Core Aesthetics
**Vibe**: Premium, modern, soft, and highly interactive.
**Layout Approach**: Floating elements, "app within an app" feel, split-screen dashboard.

## Color Palette
- **Canvas/Backdrop**: Soft warm pastel orange/beige gradient (`#FDF1E5` to `#F8E0CD`).
- **Surface**: Pure White (`#FFFFFF`) or ultra-light gray (`#F8F9FA`).
- **Primary Accent**: Bright vivid Light Blue (e.g., `#38BDF8` or `#0EA5E9`) used for active states, search buttons, route highlights.
- **Secondary Accent**: Lime Green/Pale Yellow (e.g., `#D9F99D` or `#FEF08A`) used for positive status tags and distance bubbles.
- **Text Primary**: High contrast charcoal (`#111827`).
- **Text Secondary**: Soft gray (`#6B7280`).

## Typography
- **Font Family**: `Inter` or `Plus Jakarta Sans` (sans-serif, geometric, highly legible).
- **Headings**: Heavy weights (Bold/ExtraBold), tight tracking.
- **Body/Stats**: Medium weights, crisp.
- **Labels/Tags**: Small, uppercase or capitalized, SemiBold.

## Shapes & Radii
- **Main App Container**: Extreme rounding (`border-radius: 32px`).
- **Inner Panels/Map**: Soft rounding (`border-radius: 24px`).
- **Cards/Buttons/Tags**: Pill-shaped or rounded (`border-radius: 12px` to `9999px`).

## Shadows & Elevation
- **Floating Containers**: Ultra-soft, diffuse dropshadows. 
  - CSS: `box-shadow: 0px 20px 50px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.05)`
- **Interactive Elements**: Slight shadow on hover to indicate clickability.

## Spatial System
- **Padding**: Generous. Minimum `24px` padding inside cards.
- **Gaps**: Large gaps between major layout sections (`16px` to `32px`).

## Interactive States
- **Hover**: Elements scale up slightly (`scale-105`), shadows deepen.
- **Active Navigation**: Background changes to Light Blue, icon color changes to white or deep blue.
